What Is a Soul Slam?

A Soul Slam is a custom spoken-word tribute set to original music, created to honor someone you love or wish to recognize or appreciate while they are still here to hear it.

It transforms memories, stories, struggles, gifts, love, and legacy into words of affirmation, encouragement, and remembrance.

It can serve as a eulogy. But its greater purpose is to say the important things before its too late.

How It Works

Three simple steps to a timeless tribute

1

Tell us about them

You complete a guided questionnaire that gathers the living details — stories, memories, personality, birthday, sayings, quirks, favorite songs or music, meaningful moments, the quiet truths only close people know, and the emotional themes you want the tribute to carry.

2

We go to work

Through empathetic listening and intuitive storytelling, D. Votino finds the emotional thread beneath your stories and shapes it into a spoken-word tribute that honors the person's essence. After your approval, he narrates and produces the final piece in the musical style you choose.

3

You receive the piece

You receive a polished audio tribute created to be downloaded, played, gifted, shared, and kept forever — a personal remembrance in words and music that can live with you and those you love, to return to on any special occasion or moment when love needs a voice or benediction.

FAQ

Is it a roast? ▼

No. A Soul Slam can be light, playful, and funny, but it is always created to honor, never to embarrass.

Can it be for someone who has passed? ▼

Yes. Soul Slams can be created as memorials, celebrations of life, or keepsakes for families who want someone remembered in a powerful and personal way.

Can it be for someone living? ▼

Absolutely. Birthdays, retirements, anniversaries, weddings, reunions, milestone moments, or simply a chance to honor someone while they are still here to receive it.

What is a Welcome Blessing? ▼

A Welcome Blessing (also Cradle Blessing, Naming Blessing, Arrival Blessing or First Light Tribute) is a custom spoken-word tribute with original music, created to honor a newborn's arrival, name, family story, ancestral love, and the hopes carried for their life.

Can I choose the music style? ▼

Yes. Gospel, jazz, hip-hop, country, cinematic, acoustic, soulful, sacred, playful, or a custom blend that fits the person being honored. You can also leave the music up to us. We are known to do a pretty good job.

How do you write about someone you don't know? ▼

You complete a guided questionnaire with the stories, phrases, values, memories, quirks, and details that make them unmistakably themselves. Through empathetic listening and intuitive storytelling, D. Votino finds the emotional thread beneath your stories and shapes it into a spoken-word tribute that honors the person's essence. After your approval, he narrates and produces the final piece.

What exactly do I receive? ▼

You receive a completed Soul Slam: a custom spoken-word tribute performed with music and shaped around the person, occasion, and emotional tone you choose. It is delivered as a downloadable audio file.

How long is a Soul Slam? ▼

Most Soul Slams are approximately 10–12 minutes, depending on the occasion, the amount of material provided, and the emotional pacing of the piece.

Can it be funny? ▼

Yes. Humor is welcome when it comes from love. The goal is to make people smile because they recognize the person, not laugh at their expense.

Can it be spiritual or faith-based? ▼

Yes. It can be sacred, faith-centered, gently spiritual, or not religious at all. The tone is shaped around the person being honored.

Can it include inside jokes? ▼

Yes, as long as they are loving and understandable enough to serve the piece. Small personal details often make a Soul Slam feel deeply alive.

Can multiple people contribute memories? ▼

Yes. Family, friends, coworkers, or loved ones can contribute memories and details. The strongest elements are woven into one cohesive tribute.

Can I request changes? ▼

Yes. Revisions are part of the process so the final piece feels accurate, honoring, and emotionally true.

What if I don't have many stories? ▼

That is okay. A few honest details can be enough: what they love, how they make people feel, what they always say, what they have overcome, and what you or others will never forget about them.

What makes a Soul Slam different from a eulogy, toast, or tribute video? ▼

A Soul Slam blends storytelling, poetry, music, and spoken-word performance into one custom tribute with words of affirmation and encouragement. It is not just a speech and not just a song. It is an honoring made to be felt.

Can I give it as a gift? ▼

Yes. Soul Slams make meaningful gifts for birthdays, anniversaries, retirements, memorials, family gatherings, or any moment when someone deserves to be seen and celebrated.

Who performs the Soul Slam? ▼

At this time, Soul Slams are delivered as completed custom audio pieces performed and produced for you by D. Votino.
