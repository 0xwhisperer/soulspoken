What Is a Soul Slam?

A Soul Slam is a custom spoken-word tribute set to original music, created to honor someone you love or appreciate while they are here to hear it. It transforms real memories, stories, struggles, gifts, love, and legacy into words of affirmation, encouragement, and remembrance.

It can serve as a eulogy. But its greater purpose is to say the sacred thing before it becomes too late to say it to them.

FAQ

Is it a roast? ▼

No. It can be funny, but it is meant to honor, not embarrass.

Can it be for someone who has passed? ▼

Yes. Many Soul Slams are created as memorials and celebrations of life.

Can it be for someone living? ▼

Yes. Birthdays, retirements, anniversaries—any occasion to honor someone still with us.

Can I choose the music style? ▼

Yes. From gospel to hip-hop to jazz and everything in between—or a custom blend unique to them.

How do you write about someone you don't know? ▼

You complete a guided questionnaire with the real stories and details that make them unmistakably themselves.
