# Single-Page Website Plan  
## Soul Slams by D. Votino

## Primary Goal

Convert visitors into inquiries or completed intake forms for a custom Soul Slam.

## Core Message

**Soul Slams are custom 10–15 minute spoken-word tributes with original music, created to honor someone living or passed on.**

Not a roast.  
Not a résumé.  
A remembrance.

---

## 2. “What Is a Soul Slam?” Section

This section defines the term clearly.

### Header

**What Is a Soul Slam?**

### Copy

A Soul Slam is a custom spoken-word tribute set to original music. It is written to honor the soul of a person — their story, humor, presence, struggles, tenderness, fire, and the love they leave behind.

It can be created for someone living, as a once-in-a-lifetime gift, or for someone who has passed, as a remembrance piece for a family, funeral, memorial, or celebration of life.

### Key Definition Box

**Not a roast.  
Not a generic poem.  
Not a slideshow script.  
A Soul Slam is a remembrance spoken with rhythm, music, and heart.**

## 5. “How It Works” Section

Make the process feel easy.

### Header

**How It Works**

### Step 1: Tell the Story

You complete a guided questionnaire about the person — their name, birthday, life chapters, personality, struggles, humor, sayings, memories, and what you want them or the room to feel.

### Step 2: The Soul Slam Is Written

D. Votino shapes the material into a custom spoken-word tribute that honors the person’s essence, not just their facts.

### Step 3: Original Music Is Created

The piece is paired with custom music in the genre or emotional style you choose — cinematic, soulful, gospel, hip-hop, acoustic, ambient, classical, country, jazz, or something else.

### Step 4: You Receive the Finished Tribute

You receive a polished 10–15 minute audio piece that can be played at an event, shared with family, gifted privately, or kept forever.

## 10. FAQ Section

This will reduce hesitation.

### Questions to include

**Is this a roast?**  
No. A Soul Slam can be funny, but it is never designed to humiliate or tear someone down.

**Can it be for someone who has passed away?**  
Yes. Soul Slams can be created for memorials, funerals, celebrations of life, or private remembrance.

**Can it be for someone still living?**  
Yes. Many Soul Slams are living tributes for birthdays, anniversaries, retirements, weddings, or friendship gifts.

**How long is it?**  
Most Signature Soul Slams are 10–15 minutes.

**Can I choose the music style?**  
Yes. You can request any genre or emotional direction.

**How do you write about someone you do not know?**  
You complete a detailed questionnaire. The more stories, sayings, memories, and emotional details you provide, the more personal the Soul Slam becomes.

**Can I request changes?**  
Yes, depending on the package.

**How is it delivered?**  
As a high-quality audio file. Optional written text or video versions may be offered as upgrades.

**How long does it take?**  
Standard turnaround could be 1–3 weeks. Rush options may be available.

# Lead Capture / Form Strategy

The main CTA should go to a multi-step form, not a giant form on the homepage.

### Best CTA Flow

Button: **Create a Soul Slam**  
Then form begins:

1. Who are we honoring?
2. What is the occasion?
3. Are they living or passed on?
4. When do you need it?
5. What tone do you want?
6. Your contact info
7. Then deeper questionnaire after deposit or consultation

Do not overwhelm people before they emotionally commit.
