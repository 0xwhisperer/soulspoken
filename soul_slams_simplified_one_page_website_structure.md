## 2. What Is a Soul Slam?

A Soul Slam is a custom spoken-word tribute set to original music. It honors a person’s story, humor, struggles, love, and legacy.

**Not a roast. Not a résumé. A remembrance.**

## 5. How It Works

Three steps only:

### 1. Tell us about them

You complete a guided questionnaire with stories, memories, personality details, birthday, sayings, quirks, and emotional themes.

### 2. We create the Soul Slam

D. Votino writes the spoken-word tribute and creates original music in the style you choose.

### 3. You receive the finished piece

You get a polished audio tribute to play, gift, share, or keep forever.
